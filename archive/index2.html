    About Me

    I am a postdoctoral researcher at School of Economics and Management, Tsinghua University, supervised by Prof. Liangjun Su. My research focuses on theoretical econometrics, factor models, and machine learning. I am also fascinated by the intersection of technology and the philosophy of science.
