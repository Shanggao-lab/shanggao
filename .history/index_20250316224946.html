    About Me

    I am a postdoctoral researcher at the School of Economics and Management, Tsinghua University, under the supervision of Prof. Liangjun Su. My research interests include theoretical econometrics, factor models, and machine learning. Additionally, I am intrigued by the intersection of technology and the philosophy of science.

    I hold a Ph.D. in Economics from Fudan University. Prior to that, I earned both my B.A. and M.A. in Economics from Sun Yat-sen University.

    I am an anti-labelist, non-perfect feminist, podcast lover, and hyper-procrastinator:)
